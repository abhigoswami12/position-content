These two are positioned using float: left;

you can see that even though the elements are spaced in the code,the space is not actually rendered on the page.This may not be a huge deal in these first examples, but...

What if I want my side by side elements to take up the full width of the parent element?I can get the width of each to 50%,right?

Wrong!Beacause,together the two inline block elements will add up to 100% of the available width,but that doesn,t account for the space between.
